THE BEST

FARMING

Farming itself is the process of finding gold, either from the jungle or kidnapping an enemy who made a mistake. You need to know Toppers, farming and ganking must be in the right position, usually you will do farming first before playing ganking. In one term, actually farming and jungling are not much different. The benchmark for the difference is only the location for farming which is in the Jungle.

MAP MASTERY

The ability to master the map or map awareness is very important to be able to know the opponent's position and predict the opponent's movement. To be able to improve the ability to master this map, of course, is to play more. Ways to be able to speed up map mastery skills such as: Check bushes, Importance of Lithowanderer, Hero map hack, Take advantage of trap skills, Conceal.

MICRO

The term Micro in Mobile Legends means the mastery of the Hero. A player who has good micro skills means he can master the hero very well, starting from the speed of the hand in using skills, the hero is strong in which lane, and so on. Players with hyper roles and gold lanes usually have better micros than their peers. Because they are damage dealers, they must be agile in and out during team fights so they are not easily kidnapped.
