    LEGENDS

  • Mobile Legend or can be called ML is a game with a type of online or online battle arena. In addition, this game is a multiplayer game which was later developed and published by Moonton, which is now a subsidiary of ByteDancLorem.

    Mobile Legends game is a MOBA game which was later designed specifically for mobile phones. In the Mobile Legends game itself, there are several roles. Each ML role has its own role and task. However, not everyone knows the role of Mobile Legends.



































THE HERO ROLE

TANK

Tank in MLBB games are usually at the forefront, this role is usually the leader of the attack and chooses the right time and situation to start the aggression.

ASSASIN

Assassin is a mid-range hero role that relies more on skill attacks than Basic Attacks and there are two types of attacks, namely Rhysical and Magic.

FIGHTER

Fighter adalah role hero jarak dekat yang mengandalkan gabungan dari Skill dan Basic Attack. Hero ini meskipun secara umum tidak memiliki kelincahan, namun ia dibekali dengan sarah dan tingkat durabilitas yang tinggi

MARKSMAN

Marksman atau sering disingkat MM adalah role hero jaarak jauh yang lebih mengandalkan serangan Basic Attack ketimbang skill, dan seranganya itu bersifat Physical

MAGE

Mage is type of role that has high magic power and high CD skills, specializing in dealing magic damage with his skills. This role also usually has low durability so it is often a target in teamfights that need protection

SUPPORT

Support adalah role hero yang memiliki skill khusus yang dirancang untuk membantu teman setim, baik itu dalam hal menyerang lawan aataupun menyelamatkan teman
